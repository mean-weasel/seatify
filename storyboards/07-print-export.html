Print & Export

Generate beautiful printable seating charts for your venue and export event data for backup or sharing with vendors.

4 steps ~1 min to complete Prerequisite: Seating completed

Open Print Preview

Click the Print button in the header to open the print preview. This shows a printer-friendly version of your seating chart with all table assignments.

Print or Save as PDF

Click Print to open your browser's print dialog. You can print directly or save as PDF. The layout is optimized for standard letter/A4 paper.

Tip: To save as PDF, select "Save as PDF" as the destination in your browser's print dialog instead of a physical printer.
