Guest Survey Collection

Create customizable surveys to collect guest preferences, dietary requirements, and relationship data. Share a link with guests and import their responses automatically.

6 steps ~2 min to complete Prerequisite: Event created
1

Navigate to Survey Builder

Click the Survey tab to access the survey builder. This view lets you create and customize questions that guests will answer.

SeatOptima

2

Add Survey Questions

Click + Add Question to create questions. Choose from multiple question types:

  • Single Select: One choice from multiple options
  • Multi Select: Multiple choices allowed
  • Text: Free-form text response
  • Relationship: Select guests they want to sit with/avoid

6

View Responses

Survey responses are automatically linked to guest records. View responses in the Survey tab or in individual guest profiles. Relationship preferences feed directly into the optimization algorithm.

Tip: Survey responses update guest dietary notes and create relationship constraints automatically, saving you manual data entry.
