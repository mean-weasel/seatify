Interactive Seating Demo

A hands-on walkthrough demonstrating how to assign 10 guests to 2 tables, then reassign them by dragging. See how guests "orbit" around tables and can be detached and moved to different tables.

8 steps ~5 min to complete Prerequisite: None
1

Create Two Round Tables

Click the Round button in the toolbar twice to create two round tables. Each table defaults to 8 seats. We'll adjust the capacity to 5 seats each in the next step.

Detach Bob from Table A

Guests are sticky but detachable. Click and drag Bob away from Table A. As you drag outward, the guest "unsticks" from the table and becomes a free-floating circle again. The table count updates to 4/5.

Reassign Bob to Table B (Swap with Jack)
