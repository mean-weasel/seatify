Guest Management

Add guests manually or import from CSV. Guests appear as draggable circles on the canvas that can be freely positioned and assigned to tables by dragging.

6 steps ~3 min to complete Prerequisite: None
1

View Guests on Canvas

In the Floor Plan view, guests appear as colorful circles on the canvas. Unassigned guests are freely positioned, while assigned guests orbit around their tables. Hover over any guest circle to see their name.

Add a Guest

Expand the sidebar by clicking it, then click + Add Guest. Enter details including name, email, company, group, and dietary restrictions. New guests appear as circles in a staging area on the left side of the canvas.

3

Drag Guests to Tables

Click and drag any guest circle toward a table. When you get close enough (within range), the table glows green to indicate it will accept the guest. Release to assign them. The guest circle will snap into an orbit position around the table.

Tip: The snap threshold is generous - you don't need to drop the guest exactly on the table, just get close enough for the green glow to appear.
4

Detach Guests from Tables

To remove a guest from a table, simply drag their circle away from the table. Once you're far enough away from any table, the guest becomes a free-floating circle on the canvas again, ready to be repositioned or assigned elsewhere.

Import Guests from CSV

For bulk import, click Import in the header and select "Import Guest List (CSV)". Your CSV should have columns for name, email, company, group, etc. Imported guests appear as circles in the staging area.

CSV Format: The first row should be headers. Common columns: name, email, company, group, jobtitle

6

Use Sidebar for Filtering

Expand the sidebar to search and filter guests. Use the search box to find guests by name, or filter by group and RSVP status. Clicking a guest in the sidebar highlights them on the canvas.

  • Search: Find guests by name, email, or company
  • Filter by group: Show only specific groups (e.g., "Bride's Family")
  • Filter by status: Show Confirmed, Pending, or Declined guests
