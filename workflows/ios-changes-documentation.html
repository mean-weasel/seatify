Why This Matters for iOS Users

Floating Action Buttons (FABs) are a Material Design pattern from Google's Android design system. They are explicitly NOT part of iOS design. iOS uses navigation bar buttons and action sheets instead. FABs can obstruct content and conflict with iOS gesture areas.
